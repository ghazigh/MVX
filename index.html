About MVX

MVX is the worldâ€™s first configurable and scalable co-simulation framework that incorporates realistic physical world simulation with a differentiable accurate ray tracing wireless simulation that generates multi-agent and multimodal datasets for AI-driven digital twin applications in vehicular communication systems.

Key Features

High-Fidelity Co-Simulation

MVX provides high-fidelity realistic co-simulation thanks to the realistic quality of the CARLA simulator and the high quality of Ray-traced wireless data of the sionna simulator.

Configurability

MVX provides a highly configurable simulation that allows all aspects of the physical world and the wireless environment to be manipulated.

Multimodality

MVX is designed for large-scale vehicular communication systems, ensuring optimal performance under heavy loads.

Database Management

Efficient data handling and management for comprehensive digital twin solutions.
